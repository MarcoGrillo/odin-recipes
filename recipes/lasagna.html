World's Best Lasagna

Description

The world's best lasagna recipe combines rich, layered flavors that harmonize perfectly in every bite. The dish starts with a hearty, slow-cooked meat sauce made from ground beef, Italian sausage, and finely chopped onions, simmered in a rich tomato base with garlic, fresh basil, and a splash of red wine for depth. As the sauce cooks, the flavors meld together, creating a deeply savory foundation. The pasta layers, perfectly al dente lasagna sheets, are alternated with creamy ricotta cheese mixed with Parmesan, fresh parsley, and eggs, adding a smooth, velvety texture that complements the robust meat sauce.

Each layer of the lasagna is generously sprinkled with a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan, which melts into a bubbly, golden-brown crust on top as it bakes. The secret to the perfect lasagna lies in letting it rest after baking, allowing the layers to set and the flavors to fully develop. The result is a hearty, comforting dish with the perfect balance of creamy, cheesy, and savory notes, making it the ultimate lasagna experience for any food lover.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce:
    • 500g (1 lb) ground beef
    • 250g (½ lb) Italian sausage (mild or spicy)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
    • 170g (6 oz) tomato paste
    • 240g (8 oz) tomato sauce
    • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons sugar
    • 2 teaspoons dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh basil, chopped (optional)
  • Cheese Mixture:
    • 450g (16 oz) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Pasta & Cheese Layers:
    • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked al dente)
    • 500g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)
  • For Baking:
    • Fresh parsley or basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  • Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
    • Add the ground beef and Italian sausage, breaking them apart as they cook. Cook until browned, about 10 minutes.
    • Stir in minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, red wine (optional), sugar, dried basil, oregano, red pepper flakes (optional), and salt and pepper to taste.
    • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes (up to 1 hour), stirring occasionally.
  • Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, fresh parsley, salt, and black pepper. Stir until smooth and well mixed.
  • Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook until al dente, following the package instructions.
    • Drain the noodles and lay them flat on a sheet of parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  • Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a large baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella over the ricotta.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce and repeat the process (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, sauce) until you run out of ingredients, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  • Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the lasagna with foil (to prevent sticking, you can lightly oil the foil or use non-stick foil).
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20–25 minutes, or until the cheese on top is melted and golden brown.
  • Let it Rest & Serve:
    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving. This allows the layers to set.
    • Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired, and enjoy!
